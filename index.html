We aim to send your products within one working day of receiving your order, and for you to receive it within 48 hours of it being dispatched. For orders being shipped to Europe, please allow two to seven working days from dispatch.

If your order tracking shows it’s been five working days or more since the last tracking scan, please do give us a shout and a member of our Customer Experience team will get back to you as soon as possible.
